This website is an Introduction to Pilestredet 35, one of the buildings in campus Pilestredet in the university of Oslomet. Therefore, i would like you, as in reader, to look forward for the contents and build-up of this website!

firste we introduce Pilestredet 35

Pilestredet 35 is the largest builiding in CAmpus Pilestreset. There are many rooms for lectures, group-rooms that can be booked in TP, a gym where both students and teachers can benifit of, a canteen with a wide selection of warm food and a lot more more.

Pilestredet 35 is located in "Pilestredet 35, 0166 OSlo", Beside Pilestredet 32 and near "det kongelig slottet."
